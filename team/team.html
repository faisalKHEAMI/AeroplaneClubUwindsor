Aero Plane Club

The Dream Team

The Lancer Aero Design Club is a student engineering club that aims to provide a resource for students to practice their engineering skills with a mindset oriented within the aerospace industry. The team competes annually at the International SAE Aero Design series against numerous universities from around the world with a unified mission to tackle real world aviation challenges.
