SAE Aero Design Competition

The SAE Aero Design competition offers engineering students real-world challenges.

Micro Class

For undergraduates and teams new to aerospace engineering, tasked with designing and flying radio-controlled aircraft to carry specified payloads within competition rules.

Regular Class

Teams compete with larger aircraft, focusing on meeting performance criteria such as payload capacity, efficiency, and aerodynamic principles.

Advanced Class

The highest level of competition, challenging experienced teams with complex missions involving autonomous flight and specialized payload delivery.
