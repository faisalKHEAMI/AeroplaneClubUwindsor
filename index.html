About US

The Lancer Aero Design is a team that competes annually at the international SAE Aero Design Series against numerous universities
from around the world. With a unified mission to tackle real-world aviation challenges.

The team is responsible for designing
and manufacturing a remote-controlled plane capable of operating within a set of constraints while carrying an assortment of out-sized cargo.

WHAT GETS US FLYING

TEACH

Provide a secondary classroom for students to obtain an on-campus experience of what it is like to work on a world-class engineering project in the field of aviation.

LEARN

Promote an interest and increase knowledge in aviation among students and within the community.

COMPETE

Demonstrate an outstanding performance at the SAE Aero Design competition.

REPRESENT

Be an extension of the university and represent our institution, sponsors, and donors in a professional capacity.
